Employee Perspective on Remote Work

Managers have been interested in remote work ever since new information and communication technologies (ICTs) were introduced. It was not a common practice in the beginning since it was seen as an employee's privilege or even a luxury. Since remote work became necessary for many businesses, attitudes towards it have altered in the wake of the COVID-19 outbreak and the ensuing time. But with its use have come a host of new, unidentified issues, such managing remote work, supervising and tracking staff performance, and providing employee support. The term "employee perspective" describes how workers interpret knowledge and encounters at work. Due to the subjectivity of perception, various employees may view the same work environment in different ways.
